The GE Pipeline

From Grammar to Quantum Circuit

  1. Define Grammar: BNF rules specify valid quantum operations
  2. Generate Population: Random integer genomes
  3. Decode: Map genomes â†’ quantum circuits via grammar
  4. Evaluate: Simulate & score (fidelity + efficiency)
  5. Evolve: Selection, crossover, mutation
  6. Validate: Test best circuits on IBM hardware

Key Insight: Evolution naturally discovers hardware-friendly patterns without explicit noise modeling
